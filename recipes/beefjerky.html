Beef Jerky

Description

There is nothing fancy about this. I am making it up off the top of my head, using a pre-made jerky seasoning I got for Christmas. This is where I should be telling stories of how when I was a young child I fell in love with jerky.

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs ground meat (beef, venision, turkey, snake, etc.)
  • 1 packet jerky cure mix
  • 1 packet jerky season mix

Directions

  1. Pre-heat smoker or oven to 185 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl
  3. Create 1/4" thick strips using jerky gun
  4. Cook jerky for 4-6 hours. Meat should be dry to the touch, but not scorched to oblivion
